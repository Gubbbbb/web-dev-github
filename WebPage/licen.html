Obtaining a Taxidermy License

In Michigan, in order to conduct a taxidermy business (of any kind), you first need a taxidermy license. In order to begin the process of getting a taxidermy license you must first complete an application for a miscellaneous license. A simple fee of $100 per every 3 years is required in order to keep your permit if it is accepted. For certain sales, dates and informations of specimens must be provided.
